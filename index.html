Star Wars

Episode VII - The Force Awakens

The ongoing saga of Star Wars. In this movie the Resistance teams up with a small group of heroes to fight the First Order in their attempt to rule the galaxy. This movie is set 30 years after the fall of the Galactic Empire. You will see the familiar faces of Han Solo, Luke Skywalker and princess Leia along with some fresh newbies.

Cast

  • Harrison Ford as Han Solo
  • Mark Hamill as Luke Skywalker
  • Carrie Fisher as Princess Leia
  • Adam Driver as Kylo Ren
  • Daisy Ridley as Rey
  • John Boyega as Finn

Reviews

★★★★☆ The Guardian

JJ Abrams banishes memories of George Lucas's prequels with this outrageously exciting and romantic return to a world you hadn't realised you'd missed so much.

The wait is over. The movie event of the year is here.
